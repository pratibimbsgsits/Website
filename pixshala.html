PIXSHALA: Enhance The Aesthetics will be a two-day photography workshop in collaboration with Canon that will teach the fundamentals of professional camera operation and will culminate in an exciting photography competition on the second day.

14 Oct - 15 Oct

Rules and Regulations

1. Participants are required to register themselves for the workshop before hand through the form provided.
2. All the participants are required to reach at Golden Jubilee Auditorium the workshop on 14th October 2022 at 4:00 PM sharp.
3. Any performance for the open mic will be auditioned by club pratibimb, onspot performances will not be entertained.
4.All the participants are required to handle the cameras with care.
5.Damage to the cameras will be participant's responsibility and they would have to pay for its compensation.
6. All the participants have to reach Silveria on 10:30 AM sharp for the contest on 15th October 2022.
7. No food will be provided during the lunch break, the participants are required to bring their own refreshments.
8. Any kind of improper behaviour will lead to direct termination from the workshop as well as the contest.
9. Participants are required to take the photographs for the contest during the given time, any kind of forgery or plagiarism will result in disqualification.
10. Deadline given for the submission should be followed, and entries after the deadline will not be accepted.
11. If the participants wish to take advantage of Club Pratibimb's membership, they shall have the Membership ID.
12. Final result would be based on judges decision, Club Pratibimb's call would be final and non-debatable.
