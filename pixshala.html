PIXSHALA: Enhance The Aesthetics will be a two-day photography workshop in collaboration with Canon that will teach the fundamentals of professional camera operation and will culminate in an exciting photography competition on the second day.

14 Oct - 15 Oct

Rules and Regulations

1. Participants are required to register themselves for the workshop before hand through the form link provided.
2. All the participants are required to reach Golden Jubilee Auditorium for the workshop on 14th October 2022 at 4:00 PM sharp.
3.3.Any kind of improper behaviour will lead to direct termination from the workshop as well as the contest.
4.Participants will be provided cameras for hands on experience. They are required to handle the cameras with care. Participants will have to pay for compensation if any damage is caused.
5.All the participants have to reach Silveria on 10:30 AM sharp for the contest on 15th October 2022.
6.Participants are required to take the photographs for the contest during the given time, any kind of forgery or plagiarism will result in disqualification.
7. Any performance for the open mic will be auditioned by club pratibimb, onspot registrations will not be entertained.
8. Participants are required to use all the props with care, any damage to the props or college property will be heavily fined.
9. Deadline given for submission of photographs of competition should be followed, and entries after the deadline will not be accepted.
10. Cleanliness is required to be maintained, everyone is required to throw any food wrapper or any waste in the dustbins.
11. Final result would be based on judges decision, club pratibimb's call would be final and non-debatable.
12. If the participants wish to take advantage of Club Pratibimb's membership, they shall bring the membership id.
