Graffathon

Back to rituals


Club Pratibimb is back again with the most awaited offline event, Graffathon to cheer your souls.

Graffathon is a 30 hour overnight wall painting event, that will drive you past the arena of street art and let your ideas and imagination flow on the sombre walls of our college as strokes of dexterity.

It's the time to brush up your skills, soak up your paints and get a chance to win cash prizes worth 15k.


Schedule: 5:00 PM, 11th March to 9:00 PM, 12th March 2022 Venue: Director Office Road, SGSITS

To be Noted.

  • A team wishing to register can consist of participants varying from 5-8 members. The registration cost for the same is 150 INR per team.
  • Event will commence in the following manner:
    i. Registration of teams from 25/02/2022 to 05/03/2022 (5 PM IST).
    ii. Submission of artwork by each team on A-4 sized sheet, painted, before 9th March. Artworks made, should be on one of the following themes:
    a. Urban Art
    b. Concept Art (Social cause)
    c. Innovation
    iii. The selected teams will be assigned their respective walls at 5 PM, 11/03/2022.
    iv. All the required items for graffiti making will be provided to the selected teams with a payable cost of 600 INR per team.
  • Participants are asked to report at 4 PM, 11/03/2022. All the necessary details would be mailed to the team leader.
  • Criteria for selecting top 3 winners and 2 special mentions:
  • Clarity of the theme to the viewers.
  • Creativity and originality of the artwork.
  • Final decision would be based on voting and judges’ decisions. Club Pratbimb’s call would be final and non-debatable.

    Participants are required to:
  • The covid norms i.e., wear a face mask during the entire event duration and maintain social distancing in the arena.
  • Submit their vaccination certificate at the time of artwork submission(2(ii)).
  • If the participants wish to take advantage of Club Pratibimb’s membership, all the teammates shall have the membership ID.
  • The registration fee is non-refundable; i.e if the artwork is not selected the money won’t be refunded to the team.
  • Everyone will be confined in the campus overnight and must be prepared for the same. No refreshments will be provided overnight during the event. However, food stalls will be present.
  • All the participants are to carry their college id cards and registration id (will be sent on WhatsApp to the team leader) and remain in the event premises throughout the event. Any sort of misconduct will not be tolerated and will result in direct elimination from the event.
  • Any damage to the props or college property would be heavily fined.
